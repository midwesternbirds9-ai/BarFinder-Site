Support

We've got your back 🙌

Have a question or running into an issue? We're a small team that cares deeply about your experience. Reach out and we'll get back to you quickly.

📧 support@barfinderapp.com
How does BarFinder find bars near me?
BarFinder uses your device's location (with your permission) to show bars within a radius you can customize. Location is only used while the app is open.
Is BarFinder free to use?
Yes! BarFinder is free to download and use. Some premium features may be available via an optional subscription in the future.
How do I submit or correct a bar listing?
Tap the "Suggest an Edit" button on any bar's detail page, or email us at support@barfinderapp.com and we'll update it promptly.
The app is crashing or not loading — what do I do?
Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, delete and reinstall it from the App Store. Still stuck? Email us with your device model and iOS version.
How do I delete my account and data?
You can request account deletion at any time by emailing us at support@barfinderapp.com with the subject "Delete My Account." We'll process it within 7 business days.
